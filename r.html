What Determines a Vehicle's Value?

Not all vehicles are created equal. Some depreciate quickly, while others retain their value over time due to specific characteristics. But what exactly influences a vehicle's resale value? In this project, we analyze vehicle data to uncover key factors affecting resale prices. Using advanced clustering techniques like K-Means and DBSCAN, along with correlation analysis and trend visualization, we group similar vehicles and identify patterns that drive their selling price. Through these analyses, we aim to provide actionable insights into what makes a vehicle valuable in the market.
